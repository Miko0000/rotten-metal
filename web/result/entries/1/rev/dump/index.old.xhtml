Overview

Dreammaker is a hidden international organization specialized in handling unnatural phenomena, with the purpose of utilizing and protecting the world from dangers.

While the central part of organization itself is hidden and unknown to the most but only select parts of the organization, many of it's branches are not as hidden, taking root within parts of society, expanding it's influence, using the influence it had gained to help in performing it's duty.

History

{{ block-date | 1232 | | | BRC }} Dreammaker was founded 2665 years before the New Promise. It began as a secretive government office of the {{link|missing|Ancient Reindom Empire}}, working under the emperor's direct order, making use of various controlled unnatural phenomenon to assist the empire, making it one of the very few most powerful empire at the time.

After the {{link|missing|reformation of Reindom Empire}}, Dreammaker separates itself from the governing body and becomes a {{link|missing|knight order}} to further expand it's control and operation globally.

By year 457, Dreammaker had expanded it's network to every major cities around the world.

On the year 1433, parts of Dreammaker separates itself from the majority after the {{level|5| {{link|missing|New Promise}} }}, intending to remain strict to the original code of the order. The separated group will then become the Order of Dreammaker, while the original body ceased to be a knight order.

On the year 1593, this site is created.

Overview

Dreammaker is a hidden international organization specialized in handling unnatural phenomena, with the purpose of utilizing and protecting the world from dangers.

While the central part of organization itself is hidden and unknown to the most but only select parts of the organization, many of it's branches are not as hidden, taking root within parts of society, expanding it's influence, using the influence it had gained to help in performing it's duty.

History

Dreameater, a pitch black mass seeking to devour whatever's within it's grasp. It's presence dates back from before the mythical age, when supposedly a cosmic tear leaks a pitch black liquid that landed on the {{rename|Yalmo'ord}} mountain range.

When one's body perishes, the rest of them left their body to fulfill it's promise to the world. After that, a new body is created to hold their mind, without their past memories except a little and what they're willing to afford, in the form of dreams and reminiscence.

They keep going, one body after another, bringing their dreams with them as a companion for their journey through what appears to be eternity, and eternity it seems.

Until they fell prey to the black mass that devours everything. Their body is devoured, trapped in a dark place with seemingly no escape, alongside their mind. It cannot escape the pitch black walls that surrounds it, forever become one with the ever-growing mass.

That's where their journey ends, their mind, their body, their dreams. Devoured by a Dreameater.
